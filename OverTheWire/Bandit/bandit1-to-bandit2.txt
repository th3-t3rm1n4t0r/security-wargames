Here, the password is stored in a file called '-'. If you try to run 'cat -', you might see that the terminal has become non-responsive. To stop it, just press 'Ctrl-C'. Even "cat '-'" won't work for similar reasons. The solution here is as simple as adding './' before '-'. So, you just have to type "cat ./'-'" in the terminal to get the password.
