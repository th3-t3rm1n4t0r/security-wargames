Right, now let's look at how I cracked the password of bandit3 from bandit2. This is also pretty straight-forward. All you have to do is enclose the entire name of the file in '' or "" and precede the filename with './'. So, "cat ./'--spaces in this filename--'" gives you the password. The first few levels are very easy if you have basic knowledge. The rest of the levels can be cleared by reading the man-pages of someo of the commands suggested on the page itself.
